Value Betting

The most popular AI77 strategy

A value bet is a wager where the probability of an outcome, based on your own analysis, is higher than the probability implied by the bookmaker’s odds.

Example: odds of 2.50 imply 40%. If your analysis says the real chance is 50%, that is a value bet.

  • Compare analysis with bookmaker odds
  • Use daily match predictions as a starting point
  • Stay careful with fake paid systems and scams

Bankroll Management

One of the most important skills in betting

Proper money management prevents rapid losses and increases long-term profitability.

  • Use a fixed or adjustable percentage of bankroll
  • Do not risk too much at once
  • Adjust stakes as bankroll changes
  • Track every bet and control emotions

Fibonacci & Martingale

Progression systems with real risk

Both systems can recover losses in theory, but both require discipline, limits, and a proper understanding of variance.

  • Fibonacci grows more slowly than Martingale
  • Martingale doubles after each loss
  • Both can become dangerous during long losing streaks
  • Neither guarantees profit
